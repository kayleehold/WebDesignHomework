Evermore Album

"Evermore" is the ninth studio album by Taylor Swift, released on December 11, 2020. It is a sister album to her previous release, "Folklore."

The album features collaborations with artists such as Bon Iver, Haim, and The National, and explores themes of love, loss, and longing.
