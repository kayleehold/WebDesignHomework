Why Trust Me?

 
                Hey there! I'm Kaylee, a third-year Media Arts student at the University of Montana, 
                with a deep-rooted love for music and the electrifying atmosphere of concerts. Hailing from Missoula, Montana, 
                I've been surrounded by the stunning landscapes and vibrant community that inspire my creativity. When I'm not 
                immersed in design projects, you'll often find me lost in the melodies of live music or soaking in the energy 
                of a thrilling concert. Join me as I blend my passion for design with the rhythm of life, crafting experiences 
                that resonate with the soul. Welcome to my creative journey!
