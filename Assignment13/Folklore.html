Folklore Album

"Folklore" is the eighth studio album by Taylor Swift, released on July 24, 2020.

The album is characterized by its indie folk and alternative rock sound, featuring collaborations with Aaron Dessner of The National, Jack Antonoff, and Bon Iver.
